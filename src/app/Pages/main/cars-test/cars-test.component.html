Saloon Taxis: Popular and Economical

The saloon (sedan) minicab is our most popular and economical choice, offering a versatile, all-purpose option for up to four passengers. With ample space for moderate luggage, it’s perfect for airport transfers, business trips, or local journeys.

If you have more luggage than a saloon taxi can accommodate, consider our estate taxi (station wagon), which provides extra room for larger bags or bulky items. For groups larger than four people, we recommend hiring an MPV (minivan) taxi to ensure everyone travels comfortably.
